## 1. Executive Summary

This guide prepares the UDARA AI team to deliver a compelling, high-impact investor demonstration. The demo is designed to accomplish three objectives:

1. **Establish urgency**: Convince investors that AMR in Africa is a critical, underserved problem
2. **Demonstrate capability**: Show that UDARA AI is a working product solving a real problem
3. **Present the opportunity**: Frame the investment as a high-impact, scalable, and return-generating opportunity

The standard demo runs **10 minutes** with an additional **10-15 minutes for Q&A**. Every minute is choreographed — from the opening WHO map to the closing "ask."

### 2.2 Audience-Specific Customizations

| Investor Type | Emphasize | De-emphasize | Custom Talking Point |
|---------------|-----------|-------------|-------------------|
| Impact Investor | Lives saved, equity, AMR burden | Revenue model details | "Every $1 invested prevents $15 in AMR healthcare costs" |
| Health Tech VC | TAM ($100T), unit economics, scale path | Detailed AMR science | "10,000 CHWs × $2/month = $240K/year recurring from one country" |
| AMR Fund | Technical innovation, edge AI, resistance detection | Gamification, consumer UX | "First real-time community-level AMR surveillance in Africa" |
| B2G/Dev Partners | Government demand, policy alignment, DHIS2 integration | Revenue model | "Health ministry spends $0 on AMR surveillance at primary care level" |
| Corporate | Data value, pipeline intel, CSR impact | Startup financials | "Real-time resistance data informs your drug development pipeline" |

### 2.3 Red Flags to Avoid

| Audience | NEVER Say | Why |
|----------|-----------|-----|
| Any investor | "Nobody else is doing this" | They'll find a competitor and think you're naive |
| Impact investors | "We'll make 10x returns" | They care about impact, not just returns |
| VCs | "We want to save the world" | They care about returns, not just impact |
| AMR specialists | "AI will solve AMR" | They know AMR is complex; overselling loses credibility |
| Government | "Your current system is broken" | They may be defensive; be diplomatic |
| All investors | "We have no competitors" | Always have a competitive landscape ready |

**Script (word-for-word suggested, adapt naturally):**

> "Let me start with a number. One point two seven million. That's how many people die every year from antimicrobial resistance in Africa alone. That's more than HIV/AIDS. More than malaria. And the terrifying part? Most of those deaths happen in communities where nobody is watching.

- Point to the dark zones on the map
- Speed up slightly for the $100T stat — urgency
- End with the rhetorical question — pause 1-2 seconds before transitioning

> "We asked ourselves: what if we could put eyes in every health post? Not expensive labs. Not PhD researchers. But the 2 million community health workers who are already there. That's UDARA AI."

### Appendix C: Key Terms Glossary for Non-Technical Investors

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| AMR (Antimicrobial Resistance) | When bacteria evolve to survive antibiotics, making infections harder to treat |
| CHW (Community Health Worker) | Frontline health worker serving local communities, often with limited formal training |
| Edge AI | AI that runs on local devices (like Raspberry Pi) instead of in the cloud |
| RPi (Raspberry Pi) | A small, affordable computer the size of a credit card ($80) |
| USSD | SMS-like technology that works on any phone, even without internet (*384#) |
| NER (Named Entity Recognition) | AI technique to extract medical terms from free-text reports |
| ONNX | Open format for AI models that enables efficient edge inference |
| DHIS2 | District Health Information System 2 — the world's largest health information platform |
| TAM/SAM/SOM | Total Addressable Market / Serviceable Available Market / Serviceable Obtainable Market |
| ARR | Annual Recurring Revenue |
| Gross Margin | Revenue minus cost of goods sold, as a percentage |
| Seed Round | Early-stage investment to validate and scale a product |
| Series A | Follow-on investment to scale a proven business model |
